After you have confirmed this request, your office will receive the written purchase order consisting of the order number and further details about the customer and assessment. This office request becomes binding after you have confirmed it.

Description*:

Office Request binding for the complete audit cycle (3 years)*: please confirm and book the auditor for the complete 3 year audit cycle. The office will then be responsible for supplying a suitable auditor and agreeing on a potential audit date with the customer. (For example during the onsite audit one year in advance). The leading office is responsible for communicating the audit days each year. Exceptions may occur when a different arrangement is made, or when the customer cancelled the audit e.g. site closed etc.

Currency*:

The exchange rate at the time of invoicing will be used to invoice the leading office for lending an auditor at a rate equivalent to the agreed rate in the office request. If the local currency of the answering office is not being used in the customers contract, the amount of the invoice shall be adjusted at the time of invoicing to the equivalent value in the local currency (date of invoice ) such that the answering office does not receive a lower converted amount due to currency fluctuation. If a currency clause is already part of a customer’s contract, the clause of the respective contract will be applied.
